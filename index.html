How the Game is Played:

  • The objective of the game is to select the numbers in order starting at 1 and ending at 25. You want to move as fast as possible!
  • Upon hitting the "Start" button below, you will have 3 seconds to get yourself ready.
  • Speed is the name of the game. The better you do, the more Rupees you will be awarded at the end of your round.
  • You can use Rupees to buy cosmetic changes to the site, like a new background for the website, a different font for the numbers, etc.
